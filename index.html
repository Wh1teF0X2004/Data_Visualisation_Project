Tourism Insights: Monthly Foreign Arrivals in Malaysia

Ever Curious on the status of tourism in Malaysia?

Here we visualize State-Level Tourism Trends of Malaysia by Nationality and Gender.

State Popularity and International Arrivals in Malaysia

Discover the tourist appeal of each Malaysian state. Explore where tourists originate.

Analyze the most popular Malaysian destinations. Understand the flow of international arrivals and their impact on tourism.

This map shows the distribution of international arrivals to various Malaysian states, represented using proportional symbols. The size of each symbol indicates the volume of tourists visiting a particular state.

The flow map displays the movement patterns of tourists from their countries of origin to Malaysia. It helps visualize the paths and volumes of international visitors, providing insights into travel trends and preferences.

The bump chart illustrates changes in the ranking of states based on their popularity among tourists. It provides a dynamic view of which states have gained or lost favor over time.

Gender Breakdown and State Preferences

Analyze gender distribution among tourists.

Uncover travel patterns and highlight popular destinations.

The stacked bar chart illustrates the gender distribution of tourists across different states. It highlights gender preferences in travel destinations, showcasing the proportion of male and female visitors for each location.

This radial chart represents the gender distribution of tourists. It provides a circular overview, making it easy to understand the percentage share of each gender across different states.

The donut chart focuses on the proportions of tourists by gender. It offers a compact view of the data, providing an easy-to-digest summary of male and female tourist ratios.

Statewise Analysis by Year and Month

Explore how tourist arrivals vary by state over the years.

Gain insights into seasonal and annual trends in tourism across Malaysia.

The monthly heatmap reveals the fluctuations in tourist arrivals across different months. It highlights peak and low seasons, helping to understand the temporal trends in tourism.

The yearly heatmap showcases long-term trends in tourist arrivals across Malaysian states. It helps in understanding how tourism evolves over the years and provides insights into growth patterns.

The line chart displays the trends of tourist arrivals over time, showing variations across different states. It allows for a comparative view of the rise and fall in tourist numbers throughout the years.

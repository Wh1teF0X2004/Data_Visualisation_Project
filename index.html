Tourism Insights: Monthly Foreign Arrivals in Malaysia

Ever Curious on the status of tourism in Malaysia?

Here we visualize State-Level Tourism Trends of Malaysia by Nationality and Gender.

State Popularity and International Arrivals in Malaysia

Explore the map of international arrivals to Malaysia. Discover the distribution of arrivals across Malaysian states.

The size of each symbol indicates the volume of tourists visiting a particular state.

Explore the top 50 countries of tourist origins. Select a rank range to view countries. Zoom in or out to explore the map!

Hover your mouse over the purple lines to see the number of visitors from a particular nation. The number of tourists increases with the thickness of the lines.

Explore the popularity ranking of each Malaysian states by the volume of tourist arrivals.

Notice the changes from 2020 to 2022. Observe how the COVID-19 pandemic changed state popularity rankings.

This was mostly because of the COVID-19 lockdown. It prevented many people from leaving their country to travel to other nations.

To see the state's popularity ranking, choose it from the legend. Alternatively, you can use the slider to see ranks over time.
